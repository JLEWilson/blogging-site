Journal entry 1 10/13/21

Why did you make the decision to enroll in a coding bootcamp? What are you aiming to achieve by completing 27 weeks of technical training?

For a long time i've had a dream of being a game developer. A dream of seeing my ideas not only take form in a medium that I love, but of being able to share them with others in a way that brings people happiness, or at the very least respite. During covid we had the furtune of being able to live with my partners mother while she finished up her masters and I finally had some extra free time to be able to work towards that dream. In doing so I found out just how much there was to learn. I also found out the hard way how easy it is to spend tens of hours learning the wrong thing. One thing however that I did not expect was how much I would enjoy coding! I decided I would love to code for work even if it didn't end up being for game developement.

Do you already have specific goals for yourself in mind? If not, can you think of any now?

A long term goal of mine is to open small gaming studio where we can work on material that is more meaningful without having to endure the common pitfallls that plague the video game industry. In the short term, my primary goal is to learn as much as I can and meet great people along the way! Both which have started happening already.
